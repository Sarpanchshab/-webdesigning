CITY INFORMATION

GWALIOR

One of the most beautiful cities of Madhya Pradesh in India,1 Gwalior is very famous for its marvellous palaces, old temples and the medieval fort. Being the birthplace of the great musician Tansen, the city is also highlighted for its majestic history and rich cultural heritage.

MUMBAI

Mumbai is known for its lively and cosmopolitan atmosphere, as well as its rich history and culture. The city has long been associated with the film industry and is often referred to as the “Bollywood capital.” Mumbai is famous for its bustling markets, street food, and iconic landmarks such as the Gateway of India.
